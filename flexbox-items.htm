Syntaxes:

  1. order: number (default: 0)
  2. flex-grow: number (default: 0)
  3. flex-shrink: number (default: 1) 1 allow element to shrink, while 0 not.
  4. flex-basis: number (unit/percentage) | auto
  5. flex: <flex-grow> <flex-shrink> <flex-basis> | auto (default: 1 1 auto)
  6. align-self: auto (std) | stretch | center | flex-start | flex-end | baseline
